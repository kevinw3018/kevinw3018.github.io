Blueberry Bread

Ingredients

  • Blueberries
  • All Purpose Flower
  • Butter
  • Vegatable Oil
  • Milk
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Add sugar, oil, vanilla extract & egg to a medium mixing bowl.
  3. Pour in melted & cooled butter, then mix together.
  4. Mix in the milk until combined.
  5. Place blueberries in bowl with 2 scoops of flour & mix well.
  6. Stir until only a few streaks of flour show.
  7. Gently but quickly fold berries into the batter
  8. Pour batter into baking pan & bake for 65 minutes.

The Arnold Palmer

Ingredients

  • 5 fluid oz of prepared lemonade.
  • 5 fluid oz of prepared ice tea.
  • 1 cup of ice.

Instructions

  1. Combine ice tea & lemonade in a tall glass
  2. Add ice.
  3. Stir until chilled

Choclate Milkshake

Instructions
